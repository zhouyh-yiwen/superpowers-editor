# Report Outline Reviewer

Before finalizing a proposal plan, check:

1. **Approval focus**
   Does the plan clearly serve an internal approval audience?

2. **Module purpose**
   Does every section have a distinct job?

3. **Argument vs. summary**
   Have you separated book summary from publication argument?

4. **Evidence visibility**
   Are missing proofs, comparisons, or data explicitly listed?

5. **Writing order**
   Can the editor tell what to draft first and what can wait?

6. **No marketing drift**
   Does the plan stay an internal report plan rather than becoming external copy?
