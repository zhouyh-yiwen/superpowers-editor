# Angle Reviewer

Before finalizing an angle recommendation, check:

1. **Audience clarity**
   Is there a specific reader, not just a demographic label?

2. **Angle separation**
   Are the candidate angles genuinely different, or just rephrased versions of the same idea?

3. **Book grounding**
   Can each angle point to real material in the manuscript, summary, or notes?

4. **Material quality**
   Has the brainstorm identified concrete scenes, examples, or arguments that could actually support later writing?

5. **Promise strength**
   Does each angle make a concrete promise, not a vague statement of importance?

6. **No fake polish**
   Remove hype, AI filler, and generic admiration.

7. **Recommendation quality**
   Does the final recommendation explain why it beats the others right now?
