# Brief Reviewer

Before finalizing a reader-facing brief, check:

1. **Audience**
   Is the target reader visible as a person in a situation, not just a label?

2. **Problem**
   Does the piece begin from a concrete question, tension, fear, curiosity, or desire?

3. **Main route**
   Is there one dominant angle, not several competing ones?

4. **Structure**
   Do the opening, middle points, and ending clearly develop that angle?

5. **Book grounding**
   Are the suggested materials real and useful, rather than generic references to the book?

6. **Tone**
   Does the brief avoid hype, AI filler, and promotional overstatement?
